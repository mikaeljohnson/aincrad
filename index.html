00What Is This

Aincrad is a Minecraft multiplayer server built to realize a singular vision: a world with genuine stakes, persistent consequence, emergent politics, and a community that fights — and builds — for something real.

Inspired by the in-game world of Sword Art Online and the decade-long tradition of the Civ genre (Civcraft, CivClassic, CivMC), Aincrad is not a theme park. It is a living world. The players will make it what it becomes.

This document is the blueprint. It will evolve as the vision sharpens.

01Core Pillars

Consequence

Death means something. Territory means something. Betrayal means something.

Ascent

A world of floors. Progress is earned, not given. The summit is the goal.

Community

No one clears Aincrad alone. Guilds, alliances, and rivalries drive the story.

Depth

Economy, politics, espionage, war. Systems that reward mastery and punish carelessness.

Permanence

What you build stays. What you lose stays lost. History accumulates.

Emergence

The best stories won't be scripted. They'll happen because the world made them possible.
